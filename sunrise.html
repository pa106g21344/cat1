Sunrise Cafe

Nestled in the heart of Kitui, Sunsire cafe was born out of a simple love for coffee, conversation, and community.
Founded in 2020 by sammy mwendwa, the cafe started as a small family-run business with a dream of creating a warm and inviting space where people could unwind over a perfectly brewed cup of coffee.
The cafe is located next to bonus mall with a huge welcoming sign on top of it.
